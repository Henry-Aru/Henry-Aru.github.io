In May 2026, I attended **ICASSP 2026** in Barcelona. It was an incredible journey of learning, networking, and exploration.

## The Journey: Hong Kong → Helsinki → Barcelona

My flight route took me from **Hong Kong** to **Helsinki**, and then onward to **Barcelona**. The layover in Helsinki gave me a brief but memorable taste of Nordic beauty.

I attended many inspiring talks on signal processing, medical imaging, and deep learning. The discussions and exchanges with fellow researchers broadened my perspective and sparked new ideas for future work.

## Exploring Barcelona

Beyond the conference, Barcelona was a delight. From the breathtaking **Sagrada Familia** to the lively **La Rambla**, the city's unique blend of modernist architecture and Mediterranean charm left a lasting impression. The warm weather, delicious tapas, and friendly locals made every moment enjoyable.

## A Glimpse of Helsinki

Even though I only spent a short time in Helsinki during the layover, the city's clean design, peaceful atmosphere, and beautiful waterfront were truly refreshing. I hope to visit again someday for a longer stay.

---

This trip was not just about presenting a paper — it was about growing as a researcher, building connections, and experiencing the beauty of different cultures. I'm grateful for the opportunity and look forward to more adventures ahead!
